Professional brewing for bold flavour and crema-rich shots.

Pour Over

Precision brewing for clarity, balance, and nuanced flavours.

Add coffee to a filter, slowly pour hot water over it in circular motions, and let it drip into the cup.

  • Slow circular pour

French Press

Simple immersion brewing for bold and textured coffee.

Add coarse coffee and hot water, stir, wait 4 minutes, then press the plunger and pour.

  • Long steeping
  • No paper filter

Moka Pot

Classic Italian brewing with bold intensity.

Fill bottom with water, add coffee to filter, assemble, heat on stove until coffee brews into the top chamber.

  • Medium grind
  • Low heat extraction
